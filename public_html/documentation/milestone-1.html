Milestone 1 Documentation

Suzy S. Photography: Purpose, Audience, and Goal

Purpose: The purpose of this website is to showcase Suzy's photography skills.

Audience: The target audience of this website are primarily individuals who are potentially looking for a freelance photographer, as well as potential employers who may need a full time photographer. Target audience to include: individuals seeking photographs, photography enthusiasts, recruiters.

Goal: The goal of this site is to promote and advertise Suzy's photographic ability in order to convert interested individuals into clients, as well as provide a medium by which individuals and recruiters can contact her for potential opportunities.

Persona

Name: Alice Nguyen

Age: 27

Technology: Technologically literate, uses primarily IOS devices. MacBook Air, iPhone 11. Follows trends in the Apple microcosm, rarely ventures outside of Apple's sphere of influence.

Web Browser: Sticks with Safari normally, very rarely will use chrome.

Interest in photography: Casual interest, primarily interested in finding a photographer for her wedding.

Current Mood: Alice is currently frustrated and feeling overwhelmed, with so many different photographers to choose from she is feeling the affects of choice overload.

Frustrations: Alice is frustrated with other photography sites being difficult to navigate, as well as lacking information that helps individuals build trust in the photographer.

Needs: Alice needs a clear and concise way to decide on which photographer to choose, with so many choices it is important to her that the relevant information is displayed in an aesthetically pleasing and easy to digest way.

Goals: Alice is frantically browsing the internet for a photographer she can trust, she needs to be able to determine quickly if the photographer will be able to capture her special day properly. She needs enough information at a glance to make a decision.

User Story: As a user, Alice needs to be able to quickly determine whether this photographer is perfect for her wedding.
