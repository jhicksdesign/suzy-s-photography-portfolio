About Me

Hi! My name is Suzy. I'm a photographer based out of Albuquerque, NM. I was born and raised here and love taking pictures for the community. I've been in love with photography since I started in 2007. I have had the opportunity to start off with portraits of local bands and concerts, and found my passion did not stop there.

I also like to do wedding, couples, family, and individual portraits. I prefer to shoot on location in natural lighting, and I love finding new ways to make taking photos as comfortable and fun as possible.
